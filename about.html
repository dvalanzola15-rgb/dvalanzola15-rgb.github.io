About the Author

My name is David Valanzola, and I am currently a junior at Kennesaw State University. I am pursuing a bachelor's degree in Information Technology.

I selected DNS because it is a foundational Internet service that most users depend on without seeing it work. Researching the topic helped me understand how domain names, servers, caching, and security technologies work together.

Project Goals

  • Explain DNS in an organized and understandable way.
  • Use credible research and clearly separated topic pages.
  • Create a responsive website that works on desktops, tablets, and phones.
  • Provide an interactive quiz for self-assessment.
